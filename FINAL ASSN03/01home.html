Why Donate Blood?

Donating blood can help save lives. Your donation can make a difference for someone in need of a blood transfusion due to surgery, an accident, or a medical condition.

  • Save up to three lives with one donation.
  • Receive a free health screening.
  • Enjoy refreshments and a thank-you gift.
